product

## Target users
Ukrainian Instagram shop owners managing inventory via Telegram Mini App. Mobile-first, non-technical, want zero onboarding friction.

## Product purpose
SkladBase — multi-tenant inventory SaaS as a Telegram Mini App. Entry via Telegram auth, no registration. Manages products, variants, stock, reservations. Simpler than KeyCRM/SalesDrive.

## Brand personality
Editorial sport-poster. Saturated field green, cream typography, confident negative space. Inspired by Brazilian modernist/sports posters. Not safe SaaS blue, not neon-dark, not glass-dashboard.

## Design principles
See `.claude/skills/skladbase-design/SKILL.md` for the full design system.
- Green-cream inversion (green field bg, cream text; cream card surfaces with green text)
- Giant Unbounded display numbers as the brand signature
- Flat coloured blocks — no glassmorphism, no blur, no neon glow
- Motion = typography reveal (VerticalCutReveal, ScrollFloat, NumberFlow), not particles
- Brand overrides Telegram themeParams — always green-cream
